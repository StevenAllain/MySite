Research

My research is quite broad at the moment, in terms of anything to do with reptiles and amphibians; the main two areas I focus on are infectious diseases and population monitoring. These can be reflected in my publications and blog posts that can be found on my blog. I have been slowly moving more towards the infectious disease side of things over the past couple of years due to my growing interest in the subject. I find them completely fascinating and I hope to help find a way to mitigate the devestating effects that emerging infectious diseases such as the chytrid fungus (Batrachochytrium dendrobatidis) and snake fungal disease (Ophidiomyces ophiodiicola) have on susceptible populations.
